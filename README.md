# ✈️ Travel Booking Analytics using Python

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes hotel booking data to uncover customer booking behavior, seasonal demand, cancellation trends, and market performance using Python.

The analysis demonstrates how Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) can generate actionable business insights for travel companies to improve customer experience and business performance.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Study customer behavior
- Identify cancellation patterns
- Understand seasonal demand
- Generate business recommendations

## 🔍 Key Findings

- City Hotels received significantly more bookings.
- Booking demand varied across different months.
- Some customer types cancelled more frequently.
- Online Travel Agents generated the largest share of bookings.
- Longer booking lead times were associated with higher cancellation rates.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- Increase marketing during peak travel seasons.
- Strengthen partnerships with Online Travel Agents.
- Reduce cancellations through customer engagement strategies.
- Develop country-specific promotional campaigns.
- Improve customer retention with personalized offers.
